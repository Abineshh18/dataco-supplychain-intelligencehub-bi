Stakeholder Requirements Document
Dataco Supply Chain Intelligence Hub
                                                                                        
BI Professional: Abinesh
Client/Sponsor: Supply Chain Operations Director
Business problem: 
The DataCo Supply Chain Operations team requires a comprehensive BI solution to monitor and diagnose performance across its global supply chain network. 
The dataset spans 180,518 orders across 5 global markets over a 3-year period (2015–2018). T
he business is growing in revenue but experiencing a simultaneous collapse in delivery performance, margin erosion driven by uncontrolled discounting, and untapped revenue in under-engaged customer segments.

Stakeholders: 
* Supply Chain Operations Director — Primary sponsor and decision-maker
* VP of Logistics — Responsible for carrier contracts and delivery SLA compliance
* VP of Finance — Focused on margin optimization and discount policy
* Head of Customer Retention — Focused on segment loyalty and lifetime value
* Regional Managers (5 markets) — Responsible for regional performance targets


Stakeholder usage details:

The stakeholders will use this Power BI dashboard to make data-driven decisions across four key operational domains : 
* Operations Director will use the Executive Overview (Page 1) to monitor overall OTD %, revenue trends, and cross-market performance in weekly leadership reviews.
* VP of Logistics will use the Delivery Performance page (Page 2) to identify specific region-mode combinations causing SLA breaches and to build the business case for carrier contract renegotiations.
* VP of Finance will use the Profitability & Margin page (Page 3) to monitor the discount-to-margin ratio by category and flag categories where discounting is creating loss-making orders.
* The Head of Customer Retention will use the Customer & Demand page (Page 4) to track loyalty gaps between segments and measure the revenue uplift opportunity from improving B2B engagement.

Primary requirements: 

* A geospatial map visualization showing late delivery rates aggregated by country and region.
* A visualization showing OTD % and late delivery rate trends over time (monthly).
* A matrix showing late delivery % broken down by Order Region × Shipping Mode to identify root-cause combinations.
* A visualization showing revenue and profit contribution by market region.
* A visualization showing gross margin % versus average discount % by product category.
* A treemap showing revenue distribution and margin performance across all product categories.
* A visualization showing customer segment revenue share and orders-per-customer loyalty comparison.
* Gather insights about the unrealized revenue opportunity in other customer segments.
* Gather insights about the late delivery rate and its impact on customer satisfaction.
* Gather insights about categories where discount rate exceeds or approaches gross margin.
